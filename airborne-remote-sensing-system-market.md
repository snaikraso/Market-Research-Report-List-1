<p><strong>The Airborne Remote Sensing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Remote Sensing</li><li>Passive Remote Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airborne remote sensing system market is categorized into active and passive remote sensing. Active remote sensing involves emitting energy, such as radar or laser signals, which is then reflected back to sensors, enabling measurements of surface characteristics, regardless of sunlight conditions. In contrast, passive remote sensing relies on the natural sunlight reflected off surfaces, capturing data through sensors like cameras and spectral imagers. Both types serve various applications, including environmental monitoring, agriculture, and urban planning, providing crucial data for analysis.</p></p>
