<p><p>A bias cutter is a specialized tool used in the textile and apparel industry to cut fabric on the bias, which is at a 45-degree angle to the weave. The bias cutter often features adjustable blade settings for precision and efficiency in production.</p><p>The Bias Cutter Market is experiencing significant growth, fueled by the increasing demand for custom and high-quality apparel among consumers.
