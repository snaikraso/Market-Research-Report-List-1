<p><p>The Industrial Infrared Scanner market comprises two primary types: Standard Scanners and Stereo Scanners. Standard Scanners are designed for straightforward thermal imaging tasks, offering single-axis measurement capabilities, ideal for monitoring equipment temperatures and identifying anomalies in industrial settings. In contrast, Stereo Scanners utilize dual images for enhanced depth perception and 3D thermal mapping, allowing for more detailed analysis of complex surfaces and better spatial understanding. These advanced features make Stereo Scanners suitable for intricate applications in manufacturing and maintenance.</p></p>
