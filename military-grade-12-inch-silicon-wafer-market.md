<p><p>The Military Grade 12-inch Silicon Wafer market is categorized into two main types: lightly doped and heavily doped wafers. Lightly doped wafers contain fewer dopants, enhancing their electrical resistivity, making them suitable for high-frequency applications and devices requiring minimal interference. In contrast, heavily doped wafers have a higher concentration of dopants, resulting in increased conductivity, which is ideal for power devices and applications demanding rapid electronic response. Both types are critical for military-grade electronics demanding reliability and performance.</p></p>
