<p><p>The Pre-oxidation Oven for Carbon Fiber plays a crucial role in the production of carbon fibers by facilitating their oxidation process. There are three main types: </p><p>1. **Cross-Flow Carbon Fiber Oxidation Oven**: Air flows horizontally across the material, promoting uniform heating. </p><p>2. **Parallel-Flow Carbon Fiber Oxidation Oven**: Air moves in the same direction as the fibers, enhancing thermal efficiency. </p><p>3. **Down-Flow Carbon Fiber Oxidation Oven**: Air descends vertically, allowing for effective temperature control and better resin removal. </p><p>Each type has distinct advantages and applications in carbon fiber manufacturing.</p></p>
