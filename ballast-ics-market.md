<p><p>Ballast ICs are essential components in lighting applications, facilitating efficient energy management for fluorescent and LED systems. The market can be segmented into two main types: surface mount and through-hole. Surface mount Ballast ICs are compact, allowing for higher-density designs and improved performance in smaller devices. In contrast, through-hole Ballast ICs are typically larger, providing stronger connections and easier handling during assembly. Each type caters to different application requirements, balancing space constraints with assembly preferences.</p></p>
