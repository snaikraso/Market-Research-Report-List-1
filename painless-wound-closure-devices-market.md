<p><ul><li>Closure Strips</li><li>Tissue Adhesive</li><li>Sutures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The painless wound closure devices market comprises three main types: closure strips, tissue adhesives, and sutures. Closure strips are adhesive strips that bring wound edges together, minimizing trauma and scarring. Tissue adhesives are surgical glues that bond skin layers, offering a quick and pain-free alternative to traditional methods. Sutures involve stitching the wound edges with threads, providing strong and reliable closure. Each type caters to different wound types and healing needs, enhancing patient comfort and promoting efficient healing.</p></p>
