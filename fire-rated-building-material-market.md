<p><p>The fire-rated building material market consists of various types that enhance fire safety and prevent the spread of flames. Spray products deliver protective coatings. Preformed devices are installed to manage penetrations through fire-rated walls. Putty is utilized for cable and pipe penetration, while cast-in devices integrate into concrete structures. Other products encompass miscellaneous fireproofing solutions.</p></p>
