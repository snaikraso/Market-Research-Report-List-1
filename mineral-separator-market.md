<p><p>The mineral separator market consists of various technologies, including electrostatic separation, magnetic separation, and others. Electrostatic separation utilizes differences in electrical conductivity to separate minerals, making it effective for non-metallic minerals. Magnetic separation employs magnetic fields to extract ferrous and paramagnetic materials from ores, enhancing material purity. "Others" encompasses methods like gravity separation and flotation, which rely on physical and chemical properties to sort minerals. Together, these technologies optimize mineral processing and resource recovery across various industries.</p></p>
