<p><strong>The Mineral Insulated Metal Sheathed (MIMS) Thermocouples Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grounded Type</li><li>Ungrounded Type</li><li>Exposed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mineral Insulated Metal Sheathed (MIMS) thermocouple market includes three primary types: grounded, ungrounded, and exposed. Grounded types provide rapid response times due to direct contact between the sensing element and the sheath, making them suitable for dynamic applications. Ungrounded types feature an insulated element, offering electrical isolation, which is ideal for high-voltage environments. Exposed types allow direct interaction with the measured medium, enhancing responsiveness, and are commonly used in applications requiring immediate temperature readings. Each type serves specific needs based on response time and electrical safety.</p></p>
