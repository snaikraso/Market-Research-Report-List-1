<p><p>The EPDM compounding market is segmented into three primary types: block type, particles/crumb type, and powder type. Block type EPDM is solid and commonly used for flooring, roofing, and automotive parts due to its durability. Particles or crumb type EPDM, derived from recycled material, is often utilized in athletic tracks and playgrounds for enhanced safety and cushioning. Powder type EPDM is a fine granular form utilized in various applications, including coatings, adhesives, and specialty formulations, offering versatility and improved processing properties.</p></p>
