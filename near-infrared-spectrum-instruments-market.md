<p><p>The Near Infrared Spectrum Instruments market encompasses various types including Filter Type, Dispersion Type, and Fourier Transform Type instruments. Filter Type instruments utilize optical filters to isolate specific wavelengths for analysis, while Dispersion Type instruments separate light into its component wavelengths using prisms or gratings. Fourier Transform Type instruments employ mathematical algorithms to convert raw data into usable spectra by measuring the intensity of light across multiple wavelengths simultaneously. Each type serves distinct applications in fields like agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
