<p><p>Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymers (CFRP) are advanced composite materials that combine carbon fibers with polymer matrices to enhance strength and reduce weight. The market for CFRP is primarily divided into two types: thermosetting CFRP and thermoplastic CFRP. Thermosetting CFRP, which hardens upon curing, offers superior thermal stability and mechanical properties, making it ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, thermoplastic CFRP provides flexibility in processing and recyclability, making it increasingly popular in industries such as consumer goods and sports equipment.</p></p>
