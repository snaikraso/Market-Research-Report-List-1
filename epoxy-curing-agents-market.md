<p><p>Epoxy curing agents are essential for hardening epoxy resins, and they mainly come in three types. Amine-based products, known for their fast curing times and strong adhesion properties, are widely used in coatings and adhesives. Anhydride-based products provide excellent thermal and chemical resistance, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Each type offers unique benefits, impacting the performance and durability of epoxy formulations.</p></p>
