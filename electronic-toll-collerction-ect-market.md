<p><p>Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) systems streamline tolling by automating vehicle identification and classification, enhancing traffic flow. Market types include Automatic Vehicle Identification (AVI), which uses transponders for real-time toll collection; Automatic Vehicle Classification (AVC) that categorizes vehicles based on size and type; Dedicated Short-Range Communication (DSRC) which enables vehicle-to-infrastructure communication for seamless transactions; and Video Enforcement Systems that utilize cameras for toll assessment and enforcement. Other technologies may also include mobile apps and RFID systems to support tolling operations.</p></p>
