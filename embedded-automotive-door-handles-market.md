<p><p>The embedded automotive door handles market includes two primary types: button type and induction type. Button type handles require manual pressing to unlock and open doors, offering a traditional user experience with mechanical components. In contrast, induction type handles utilize proximity sensors to detect the presence of a key fob, enabling hands-free operation; this enhances convenience and security. Both types cater to evolving consumer preferences for functionality and aesthetics in modern vehicles while reflecting advancements in automotive technology.</p></p>
