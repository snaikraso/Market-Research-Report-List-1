<p><strong>The Pediatric Spinal Orthoses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Orthotics</li><li>Static Orthotics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pediatric spinal orthoses market is categorized into dynamic and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to provide support while allowing movement, promoting normal spinal alignment and mobility in children with conditions like scoliosis. In contrast, static orthotics offer rigid support to immobilize the spine, aiding in correction and stabilization for various spinal deformities. Both types play crucial roles in pediatric care, addressing the unique needs of growing patients while improving overall quality of life and preventing further complications.</p></p>
