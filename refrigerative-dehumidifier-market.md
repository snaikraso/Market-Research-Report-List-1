<p><p>The refrigerative dehumidifier market features two main types: tower type and rotor type. Tower type dehumidifiers utilize a vertical design that maximizes airflow and moisture collection, making them efficient for large spaces. In contrast, rotor type dehumidifiers employ a rotating desiccant wheel to absorb moisture from the air, offering continuous operation with low energy consumption. Both types are effective in reducing humidity levels, enhancing indoor air quality, and preventing mold growth in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
