<p><p>The direction finding antenna market includes various types, notably dipole and monopole antennas. Dipole antennas consist of two conductive elements and are effective for detecting signals from various directions, making them ideal for applications like emergency services and navigation. Monopole antennas, on the other hand, feature a single element mounted above a ground plane, offering compact size and ease of use. Both types are essential in communications, surveillance, and military applications, providing accurate signal localization and direction detection.</p></p>
