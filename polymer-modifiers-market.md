<p><p>The Polymer Modifiers market encompasses several key types that enhance material properties. Performance-tailored tougheners improve impact resistance and durability. Coupling agents enhance adhesion between different materials, facilitating better composite performance. Flexibilizers introduce flexibility and elasticity, making polymers more pliable without compromising strength. Mixed plastics compatibilizers ensure compatibility between dissimilar plastics, promoting uniform dispersion and reducing phase separation. Together, these modifiers play a critical role in optimizing the performance and functionality of polymer-based products across various applications.</p></p>
