<p><p>The Membrane Bioreactors (MBR) market primarily features two key types: CCAS Processing Technology and Continuous Microfiltration Technology. CCAS (Controlled Aerobic Solids) Processing Technology integrates biological treatment with membrane filtration, enhancing nutrient removal and biomass retention, making it suitable for municipal and industrial wastewater applications. Continuous Microfiltration Technology, on the other hand, utilizes membrane filtration to continuously separate liquid and solids, offering efficient operation and improved effluent quality. Both types emphasize sustainability and resource recovery in wastewater treatment processes.</p></p>
