<p><strong>The Power Battery Cooling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated System</li><li>Distributed System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Battery Cooling System market comprises two primary types: Integrated Systems and Distributed Systems. Integrated Systems combine cooling components directly within the battery pack, optimizing space and enhancing thermal efficiency. They provide a streamlined design that integrates heat management into the overall battery structure. In contrast, Distributed Systems feature separate cooling units that operate independently from the battery pack. This modular approach allows for flexibility in design and can facilitate easier maintenance and upgrades, adapting to various thermal management needs.</p></p>
