<p><p>The aerospace displacement sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: linear and angular sensors. Linear sensors measure the displacement along a straight line, providing precise position data essential for applications such as landing gear and control surface monitoring. Angular sensors, conversely, measure rotational displacement or angle changes, crucial for tracking movements in components like flight control systems and engine adjustments. Both types enhance safety and performance in aerospace applications, ensuring accurate measurements for optimal aircraft operation.</p></p>
