<p><p>The Lithium-Ion Battery ICs market is segmented into two main types: Single Cell and Multi-Cell configurations. Single Cell ICs manage individual lithium-ion cells, focusing on optimizing performance, safety, and lifespan for compact devices. In contrast, Multi-Cell ICs oversee battery packs with multiple cells, providing efficient management of charging, balancing, and thermal conditions. The Multi-Cell market caters to applications requiring higher power and capacity, such as electric vehicles and energy storage systems, while Single Cell ICs are prevalent in consumer electronics.</p></p>
