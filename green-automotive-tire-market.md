<p><p>The green automotive tire market comprises two main types: all-steel tires and semi-steel tires. All-steel tires are constructed entirely of steel belts, enhancing durability, reducing rolling resistance, and improving fuel efficiency. In contrast, semi-steel tires combine steel belts with fabric layers, offering a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness. Both tire types aim to reduce environmental impact through better fuel economy and longer lifespan, contributing to sustainability in the automotive industry while meeting consumer demand for eco-friendly solutions.</p></p>
