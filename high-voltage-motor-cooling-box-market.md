Air cooling boxes utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them suitable for less demanding applications and environments with adequate ventilation. In contrast, water cooling boxes employ water circulation for more efficient thermal management, enabling higher performance in applications with intense heat generation. Both cooling methods aim to enhance motor efficiency, reliability, and longevity, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
