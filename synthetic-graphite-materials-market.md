<p><strong>The Synthetic Graphite Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isostatic Pressure Graphite</li><li>Die-Pressed Graphite</li><li>Extruded Graphite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Synthetic graphite materials are categorized based on their manufacturing processes. Isostatic pressure graphite is produced using uniform pressure, resulting in high density and superior structural integrity, ideal for high-performance applications. Die-pressed graphite is formed by compacting graphite powder in a mold, offering good mechanical properties and cost-effectiveness for various applications. Extruded graphite is created by forcing graphite paste through a mold, enabling continuous shapes and sizes, widely used in specialized industries like electrical and thermal applications.</p></p>
