Rotatable targets enable continuous deposition over a larger area, enhancing efficiency and uniformity during the sputtering process. They are suitable for high-volume applications and can reduce material waste. Non-rotating targets, on the other hand, are simpler and often more cost-effective for smaller applications or batch processes. Both types cater to various industries, including electronics and optics, where titanium dioxide is crucial for thin-film coatings.</p></p>
