<p><p>Online fundraising and donor management solutions can be classified into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing organizations to access and manage donor data and fundraising activities from anywhere with internet connectivity. This model typically offers scalability, automatic updates, and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premises solutions are installed locally on the organization’s servers, providing greater control over data security and customization, but often require more substantial initial investment and ongoing maintenance.</p></p>
