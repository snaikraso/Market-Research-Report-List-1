<p><p>The plastic prototyping service market includes several key methods for creating prototypes. Vacuum casting involves pouring liquid plastic into a silicone mold, ideal for low-volume production. 3D printing enables rapid and cost-effective prototyping directly from digital models, allowing complex designs. CNC machining uses computer-controlled tools to precisely cut plastic, ensuring high accuracy for functional prototypes. Injection molding, suited for high-volume production, involves injecting molten plastic into molds, offering durability and consistency in final products. Each method serves distinct needs in prototyping processes.</p></p>
