<p><p>The seed additives market comprises two main types: dry form and liquid form. Dry form additives, such as powders or granules, are applied during seed handling and provide essential nutrients and protection against pests and diseases. Liquid form additives, including solutions and suspensions, are often treated directly onto seeds or used in seed soaking, enhancing absorption and effectiveness. Both forms aim to improve seed performance, boost germination rates, and promote healthy plant growth, catering to diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
