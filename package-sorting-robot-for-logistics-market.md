<p><p>The package-sorting robot market in logistics is divided into two main types: fixed robots and mobile robots. Fixed robots are stationary systems designed for high-speed sorting in dedicated spaces, optimizing efficiency in warehouses. They often integrate with conveyor belts or automated storage systems. Mobile robots, on the other hand, are versatile and can navigate dynamically, moving packages throughout facilities. They enhance flexibility and can adapt to changing layouts, making them ideal for diverse logistics environments. Both types increase operational efficiency and accuracy in sorting tasks.</p></p>
