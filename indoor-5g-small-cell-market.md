<p><p>The indoor 5G small cell market is categorized into two main types: integrated 5G small cells and distributed 5G small cells. Integrated 5G small cells combine radio, processing, and backhaul components into a single unit, making installation easy and space-efficient. In contrast, distributed 5G small cells employ multiple units connected through a central coordination system, enabling extensive coverage and flexibility. Both types enhance indoor connectivity and capacity, addressing the growing demand for high-speed wireless services in dense urban environments.</p></p>
