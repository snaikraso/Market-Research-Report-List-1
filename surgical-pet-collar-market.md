<p><p>The surgical pet collar market comprises primarily two types: plastic material and fabric collars. Plastic collars, often cone-shaped, provide a rigid barrier, preventing pets from accessing wounds while remaining lightweight and durable. Conversely, fabric collars offer a softer, more comfortable alternative, allowing pets greater freedom of movement while still restricting access to affected areas. Each type caters to different pet needs, with plastic collars emphasizing durability and effectiveness, while fabric collars focus on comfort and ease of use during recovery.</p></p>
