<p><p>ICU disinfection robots are essential for maintaining infection control in healthcare settings, using technologies like UV-C and HPV. UV-C robots utilize ultraviolet light to eliminate pathogens on surfaces, providing quick disinfection without chemicals. In contrast, HPV (Hydrogen Peroxide Vapor) robots release vaporized hydrogen peroxide, effectively penetrating various surfaces and reaching difficult areas. Both methods enhance environmental safety and reduce hospital-acquired infections, making them vital in intensive care units and other high-risk medical environments.</p></p>
