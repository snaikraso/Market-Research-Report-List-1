<p><ul><li>Abrasion Deburring Machine</li><li>Electrochemical Deburring Machine</li><li>High-Pressure Fluid Jet Deburring Machine</li><li>Thermal Deburring Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deburring machine market comprises various types tailored for specific processes. Abrasion deburring machines use mechanical abrasion to remove rough edges from parts. Electrochemical deburring machines employ chemical reactions to selectively remove burrs without altering the base material. High-pressure fluid jet deburring machines utilize streams of fluid to efficiently eliminate burrs from intricate shapes. Thermal deburring machines apply heat to vaporize material, effectively smoothing edges. Each type addresses unique manufacturing needs, ensuring precision and surface integrity.</p></p>
