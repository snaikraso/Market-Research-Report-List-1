<p><p>The polyester tire fabric market is primarily categorized into all-steel tire fabric and bias tire fabric. All-steel tire fabric involves constructing tires with a steel belt, enhancing durability, strength, and puncture resistance, making it ideal for high-performance vehicles. In contrast, bias tire fabric features layers of fabric plied at angles, providing flexibility and a smoother ride, commonly used in less performance-focused applications. Both types cater to distinct consumer needs, influencing their development and usage in various tire segments.</p></p>
