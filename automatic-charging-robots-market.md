<p><p>The Automatic Charging Robots market is categorized into two main types: Vehicle Units and Offboard Units. Vehicle Units are integrated directly into the electric vehicles, allowing for on-the-go charging without the need for driver intervention. Offboard Units, on the other hand, operate externally and are often stationary, serving multiple vehicles at designated charging locations. Both types enhance charging efficiency and convenience, streamlining the process of recharging electric vehicles in various settings, from parking lots to public charging stations.</p></p>
