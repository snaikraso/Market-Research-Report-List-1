<p><strong>The Metal Stamping Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blanking Process</li><li>Embossing Process</li><li>Bending Process</li><li>Coining Process</li><li>Flanging Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal stamping products encompass various processes that shape and manipulate metal sheets. The blanking process cuts out flat shapes from metal sheets, while embossing creates raised designs. The bending process forms angular shapes by bending metal at specific points. Coining involves precision forming, imprinting designs or logos onto metal. Lastly, the flanging process involves creating a flange or lip on the edges of metal pieces for improved fit or attachment. Each method serves distinct applications in industries like automotive and manufacturing.</p></p>
